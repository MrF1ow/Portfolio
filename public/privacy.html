Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

Overview

This site is a personal portfolio for Ethan Flow. It does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics of any kind. Your privacy is respected by default.

What Data Is Collected

The only data collected is what you voluntarily submit through the contact form:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your message

How Your Data Is Used

Contact form submissions are sent directly to my email inbox via EmailJS, a third-party email delivery service. Your information is used solely to respond to your message. It is never sold, shared with other parties, or used for marketing.

Third-Party Services

EmailJS processes contact form submissions. Their privacy policy is available at emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect basic connection data (IP address) when fonts are served. See Google's privacy policy.

Cookies & Tracking

This site does not set any cookies or use any analytics or tracking tools.

Data Retention

Contact form messages are retained in my email inbox only as long as needed to respond. There is no database or server storing your submissions.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of any data you've submitted by emailing ethanflowdev@gmail.com.
